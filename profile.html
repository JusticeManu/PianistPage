In addition to all the technological challenges a artist has to contend with, the biggest challenge to conquer during a show is the fear of failing before an audience. Pianists in particular have to struggle with memorization, because they are expected to learn the entire concert by heart nowadays.

The fear of playing incorrect notes, or even stopping them, may also affect the output level. Most pianists who are unable to cope with this tension give up their performing career, choosing to teach rather than act. At the other hand, anticipation may have a positive impact at play as it brings more motivation and adrenaline to the artist.
